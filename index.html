Our projects

Our lab is affiliated with the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, located at the foot of Luojia Mountain. With the focus on Natural Language Processing and Graph Data Management, we are particularly interested in the following topics:

  1. Causality for NLP
  2. Alignment of Large Language Models
  3. Integration of Large Language Models with Knowledge Graphs
  4. Ensuring the Safety of Large Models
  5. Graph Processing
  6. Graph Data Management
Our research aims to push the boundaries of how machines understand and interact with human, while also ensuring the ethical and safe deployment. Through our efforts, we hope to contribute to the development of intelligent systems that can address complex real-world problems.
